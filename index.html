Phase 5 · Sep 13 Onward

Ski Transition

The aerobic base doesn't change — it just gets a new direction. Uphill Athlete: the Transition period "prepares the body for the harder training ahead." Jimmy Pritchard (SSCV, Mikaela Shiffrin's S&C coach): ski-specific strength built 8–12 weeks before first snow sets the foundation for the entire ski season.

The through-line: The Z2 base you built chasing MTB rides and peak bags is the same aerobic engine that powers long skin tours and hard downhill days. You don't rebuild — you redirect. Reduce bike/run volume gradually through October. Hold strength and aerobic base through November. Hit December's first snow ready.
